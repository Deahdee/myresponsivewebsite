Evolving Landscape:
Infrastructure Shift Trough Five Decades

Main Objective

To trace and showcase the transformation of the campus’ infrastructure from 1985 to 2025, highlighting key developments and the factors that influenced physical growth and modernization.

Scope

This project focuses on the physical changes within the campus, specifically the construction, renovation, and expansion of buildings and facilities over four decades.

Significance

This project offers a deeper understanding of how the campus has grown to meet the evolving needs of its community. It helps preserve institutional memory, supports future planning, and strengthens appreciation for the school's journey and progress.
